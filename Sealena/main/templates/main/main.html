Welcome

Sealena

Sealena, it's Medical Information Management and Collaboration Software created with the purpose of easing the collaboration between health workers, the organization and accessing of information from anywhere

Some of the features included in the Premium Version:

  • Patients and Agenda Management
  • More than 1 assistant linking
  • Unlimited amount of created patients
  • Unlimited amount of appointments

Manage all your information

Management

Patiends, Consults, Appointments and even Providers, everything can be managed with Sealena. Our main goal is to provide you a centralized software where you can manage all your information and be able to access it wherever you are.

Connect with your colleagues

Collaboration

You can connect and contact all your colleagues who have a Sealena account. Be able to collaborate with them and share ideas with the Sealena's Chat System.

Link to your assistants

Linking

You may not want to fill up the appointments by yourself. No problem, with Sealena you can link different assistants to your account through the unique Linking ID and let them create, confirm, update or cancel appointments for you.

Alive Data

Statistics

You want to see how your data is behaving? No problem, we got you covered. Sealena provides you with realtime Statistics based on the data you are managing and the data you desire to take a look at.

Remind your patients

Reaching

Your patients will never miss an appointment with Sealena's Messaging System. Whenever a consult has been created for the specified patient, a direct message will be sent with the exact date and time of his/her appointment.

Mail your patients and providers directly

Mailing

Have a direct contact with your patients or ask your providers for a specific product, all directly from Sealena. Sealena's mailing system will be responsible to make your emails arrive instantly and securely to your target.
